Sugar snap pea and blueberry salad

When you combine tender sugar snap peas with fresh blueberries, a fruity dressing, and a bit of crunch from the almonds, you're in for a treat. This will make 6 side salads or 4 main course salads.

Ingredients

Salads:
    1 pound sugar snap peas
    1 ½ cup arugula-baby spinach combo
    ½ cup fresh blueberries

Dressings:

    ½ cup vegetable oil
    ½ cup fresh blueberries
    2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
    1 tablespoon honey
    1 tablespoon slivered almonds

Directions

    Step 1 Cut ends off snapp peas and pull off the tough strings running up the sides.
    Step 2 Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add snap peas, cover, and steam for 1 minute or until you reach desired tenderness. Don't overcook because you still want a little crunch.
    Step 3 Transfer peas to a bowl of ice water for a few minutes to stop the cooking and retain the bright green color; drain well. Transfer peas to a large bowl and add arugula-spinach combo and ½ cup blueberries
    Step 4 Combine oil,blueberries, vinegar, and honey for a dressing in a food processor or blender, process until smooth.
    Step 5 Drizzle dressing over salad mixture and toss untill well coated. Sprinkle almonds over the top and serve.
